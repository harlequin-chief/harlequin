# RESULTS — Harlequin reputation engine prototype
> Generated by `run_all.py` (stdlib only). Reproducible: seeded PRNG. The figures are
> **relative** (power distribution), not absolute units. Maps SPEC §1 (reputation), §1.6

## Thesis under test
**The real anti-Sybil wall is EARNED reputation, not the personhood test** (SPEC §1.5, §2.4).
Creating fake identities or farming vouches in a circle must NOT grant structural power,
because reputation is anchored in real evidence and collusion is damped (§1.6).

**Reading:**
- **Sybil:** 200 fake accounts (40% of the network) capture **0.00%** of the reputation and **0.00%** of the consensus committee. They are born with reputation 0; without evidence nor vouches from the reputed, they gain no power.
- **Collusion:** the ring of 30 vouching in a circle captures **3.94%** of the reputation and **0.00%** of the committee, despite the 3 fooled honest members vouching for it.

## 2. How much does anti-collusion (damping, §1.6) contribute?
**Reputation-laundering** attack: a ring member (`c0`) DOES have high legitimate reputation
(real work) and tries to **split it** among 29 cronies by vouching in a circle. The damping
must stop the propagation: `c0` keeps its own, the cronies stay at ~0.

| | `c0` reputation (legitimate) | Split to the 29 cronies | Max of one crony |
|---|---|---|---|
| **WITHOUT damping** | 83.7 | 213.9 | 17.5 |
| **WITH damping** | 133.2 | 24.6 | 12.7 |

The damping cuts the reputation laundered to the cronies **8.7×**.
Without anti-collusion, a single member with real reputation can 'lend' it to its whole
ring of puppets; with it, reputation stays where it was earned.

## 2b. Open frontier: SOPHISTICATED collusion (scattered ring, §1.6)
The dense clique is easy to detect. A **scattered** ring (each colluder vouches for few, low reciprocity/overlap) mimics honest patterns and **slips through** the local damping. We test a new defence: **community detection** (the global signal the scattered ring does leave).

| Defence on the scattered ring | Laundered to the cronies |
|---|---|
| no damping (reference) | 209.5 |
| LOCAL damping (pairwise independence only) | 148.0 |
| LOCAL + COMMUNITY damping (new) | 66.2 |

(Reference: the dense clique with local damping launders only 24.6.)

**Result:** community detection reduces the scattered ring's laundering from **148** (local damping) to **66** (~2.2× less) — it closes much of the gap the local damping left.
**False-positive control:** total honest reputation barely changes with the community defence (-0.1% over the base) → it does not punish honest communities (they have real evidence, lowering their suspicion). Honest: still opt-in and to validate further; adaptive collusion (fragmenting the ring into several communities) is the next frontier (§1.6, PAPER §10).

## 2c. Open frontier: ADAPTIVE collusion (fragment to evade, §1.6)
The attacker knows we punish dense-without-evidence communities, so it **fragments** the ring into small scattered sub-rings to fall below the radar. But to launder c0's real reputation to the puppets, it must **flow** between fragments through a few bridges. That is the tension: evading the label chokes the flow.

| fragments | communities seen | laundered no damping | laundered +community | **consensus power (min)** |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 1 | 209.5 | 66.2 | **0.000** |
| 2 | 2 | 214.8 | 61.3 | **0.000** |
| 3 | 3 | 239.3 | 62.1 | **0.000** |
| 5 | 5 | 200.9 | 53.2 | **0.000** |
| 6 | 6 | 209.4 | 56.0 | **0.000** |
| 10 | 6 | 264.6 | 42.4 | **0.000** |
| 15 | 3 | 202.9 | 49.2 | **0.000** |

**Result (two-fold):** (1) fragmenting **raises** the number of communities seen (evades the label) but does **not raise** the laundering under defence — as it fragments, the bridges become bottlenecks the LOCAL damping bites harder; the worst case for the defence is the scattered ring WITHOUT fragmenting. (2) What does leak is **single-dimension** (only `commerce`): under the conservative aggregate (min, §1.2b) that governs consensus/vouch power, the puppets collapse to **~0** at any fragmentation. For real power the attacker would need verifiable evidence in **every** dimension for each puppet = doing the honest work. *Diffuse laundering buys no structural power.*

## 2d. Open frontier: ASYMMETRIC collusion (PageRank funnel, §1.6)
Instead of a reciprocal ring, a **directed funnel**: many feeders (with some real work) all vouch for the same target c0 (evidence 0), without reciprocity, to concentrate/funnel their reputation onto c0. **Honest finding:** the funnel **evades the local damping** — since c0 vouches for nobody, neighbour overlap is 0 and reciprocity is 0, so `independence(feeder→c0)=1` and the damping does not cut the pump.

| feeder diversification | c0 no damping | c0 +local damping | c0 +community | **c0 consensus power (min)** |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 0 | 206.9 | 240.8 | 228.5 | **0.000** |
| 3 | 63.7 | 76.4 | 69.7 | **0.000** |
| 6 | 37.8 | 45.4 | 39.2 | **0.000** |

**Result (two-fold, as in §2c):** (1) the graph damping does **not** stop the funnel — a real gap: local independence looks at reciprocity and overlap, signatures the funnel does not leave. (2) But the pump is **single-dimension** (only `commerce`): under the conservative aggregate (min, §1.2b) c0's consensus power collapses to **~0**. *The vectorial aggregate is the backstop where the graph damping does not reach.* For real power, c0 would need evidence funnelled in **every** dimension = the feeders doing real work in all of them and ceding it = honest work. **Live frontier:** harden independence with an **in-degree-concentration-from-one-community** signal (with false-positive analysis on legitimately popular honest nodes) — next engine iteration.

## 4. Persistent liability + cascade slashing (§1.5c, §1.7)
The protege defrauds and loses 100. The hit climbs the vouch chain (½ per hop).

| Agent | Before | After | Δ |
|---|---|---|---|
| protege | 100.0 | 0.0 | -100.0 |
| middle | 120.0 | 70.0 | -50.0 |
| mentor | 200.0 | 175.0 | -25.0 |
| outsider | 150.0 | 150.0 | +0.0 |

Whoever vouches answers for whom they let in, even later. The `outsider` (did not vouch) is
unaffected. Sponsoring carelessly is costly -> it forces selectivity.

## 5. Vouch economy (§1.5c)
**Live-vouch quota = sublinear function of reputation** (decreasing returns):

| Reputation | Vouch quota |
|---|---|
| 1 | 3 |
| 10 | 10 |
| 100 | 19 |

**Mentor dividend:** sponsoring someone with real independent reputation yields 4.00; sponsoring a puppet (independent reputation ~0) yields 0.03. Sponsor->puppet farms do not pay off (§1.6).

## 6. Temporal dynamics: decay and anti-entrenchment (§1.7, Art. VI)
The base engine is a fixed snapshot; here TIME is modelled in epochs by aging the evidence anchor (ρ=0.7 retention per epoch). Uncontributed reputation evaporates (§1.7) and yesterday's power does not shield tomorrow's (Art. VI).

| 2 | 283 | 415 | 436 | 22 |
| 3 | 364 | 372 | 359 | 19 |

| 6 | 553 | 274 | 188 | 9 |
| 7 | 594 | 253 | 152 | 7 |
| 8 | 626 | 236 | 124 | 5 |
| 9 | 650 | 224 | 104 | 4 |

**Result:** whoever **keeps contributing** holds and grows; whoever **retires** decays (~46% from its peak) → anti-entrenchment (Art. VI). A **pioneer** of a single work deflates (~87%) → **free anti-long-range** defence: an old history is not reactivated into power. And a **farm** that farms and sits sees its puppets evaporate: collusion has to be SUSTAINED, not a sprint.

## 7. Protege graduation: sponsorship is scaffolding (§1.5c)
A protege enters sponsored (its reputation leans on the mentor vouch) and, as it does real work and receives independent vouches, it graduates: the mentor vouch is released and stops taking up its quota. The liability persists (cascade slashing). We measure A's reputation WITH vs WITHOUT the mentor vouch (the independent one).

| epoch | A total rep | A independent rep | % independent | M free quota | graduated |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|:--:|
| 0 | 162 | 0 | 0% | 22 | — |
| 1 | 216 | 76 | 35% | 22 | — |
| 2 | 276 | 158 | 57% | 21 | — |
| 3 | 315 | 219 | 70% | 22 | yes |
| 4 | 371 | 290 | 78% | 21 | yes |
| 5 | 425 | 357 | 84% | 21 | yes |
| 6 | 468 | 414 | 88% | 20 | yes |
| 7 | 512 | 464 | 91% | 20 | yes |

**Result:** A starts dependent on the scaffolding (independent rep ~0) and at **epoch 3** stands on its own (≥60% independent) → it **graduates**, freeing the mentor's quota (21→22). Sponsoring done well invests in the protege becoming **independent**, not in tethering it; the scaffolding is designed to dilute (consistent with the genesis seed and Art. VI).

Anti-entrenchment in the trust graph too.

## Conclusion
The prototype confirms, in figures, the SPEC's central bet: **structural power is not bought
with identities nor with inbred vouches**. Sybils and collusion rings stay near 0% of consensus
power; the anti-collusion damping is measurable and necessary; whitewashing does not pay off;
and persistent liability makes collusion expensive. Sophisticated collusion — scattered ring
(§2b) and fragmented/adaptive (§2c) — buys no power either: what leaks is single-dimension and
the conservative aggregate (§1.2b) nullifies it. Live frontiers: hardening independence against
the asymmetric funnel (§2d) and a formal safety/liveness proof of the consensus.
